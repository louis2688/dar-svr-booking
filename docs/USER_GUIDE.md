# Service Vehicle Request (SVR) — User Guide

**Department of Agrarian Reform · Marinduque**

A plain-language guide to booking a government vehicle, tracking your request,
and reading the calendar. No technical knowledge needed.

- **Website:** https://svr-booking-dar.vercel.app
- **Times:** everything is shown in **Manila time (Asia/Manila)**
- An in-app version of this guide is available under the **Help** menu, and as a
  printable page at `/user-guide.html`.

---

## 1. What this system is for

The SVR system is how staff of the DAR Provincial Office in Marinduque ask to
use an office vehicle for official travel. Instead of paper slips, you fill out
a short online form. An administrator reviews it, assigns a vehicle, and
approves it. You can then print the official request form, and everyone can see
which vehicles are already booked on a given day.

- **Regular users** can create requests, follow their status, and view the shared calendar.
- **Administrators** can do all of that *plus* assign vehicles, approve or reject requests, and manage the office records.

## 2. Signing in & your account

Open https://svr-booking-dar.vercel.app in any web browser, or use the mobile app (section 9).

**If you already have an account**
1. Tap **Sign in**.
2. Enter your **email** and **password**.
3. Tap **Sign in** again — you land on your dashboard.

**If you're new**
1. Tap **Sign up** on the welcome screen.
2. Enter your name, work email, and a password you'll remember.
3. Open the verification link we email you to activate your account.

**Forgot your password?** On the sign-in screen choose **Forgot password**,
enter your email, and follow the reset link. Your old password stops working
once you set a new one.

## 3. Reading the dashboard

The dashboard is your home screen. The left menu has **Dashboard**,
**Bookings**, **Calendar**, and **New Request**. At the top are four count
cards — **Pending**, **Approved**, **Rejected**, **Cancelled** — followed by a
yearly chart and a table of recent bookings.

> The dashboard shows **all** bookings in the office, not only your own. Only
> administrators see the Edit, Cancel, and Delete buttons — for everyone else
> it is view-only.

## 4. Requesting a vehicle

Choose **New Request**, then work down the form.
1. **Pick the trip date** on the calendar. Grey past dates can't be chosen.
2. **Choose a start time.** Times already taken show *(booked)* and can't be picked — choose a free one.
3. **Enter the requestor's name** — the person the vehicle is for.
4. **Type the destination** and the **purpose of travel**.
5. **Add passengers**, one name per line (optional).
6. Tap **Submit request**. You get a **control number** — write it down or screenshot it.

Your request starts as **Pending**. An administrator assigns a vehicle and
approves or rejects it. You don't pick the vehicle yourself — that happens
during approval.

## 5. What the statuses mean

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **Pending** | Submitted, waiting for an administrator to review and assign a vehicle. |
| **Approved** | A vehicle is assigned and the trip is confirmed. You can print the form. |
| **Rejected** | The request was declined. Submit a new one if plans change. |
| **Cancelled** | The booking was called off by an administrator. |

You can filter the Bookings list by status, search by control number or
destination, sort it, and choose how many rows to show per page.

## 6. The booking calendar

Choose **Calendar** to see which vehicles are busy. Pick a **vehicle** (or leave
it on **All vehicles**) and a **month**.

- **All vehicles:** a day shows how many bookings it has, e.g. *3 booked*.
- **One vehicle:** a day shows a number like *1/48*. The day is split into 48
  half-hour start times; *1/48* means one is taken.
- Colours: **green** = open, **amber** = partly booked, **red** = fully booked.

Tap any day to see the exact booked times, with the vehicle and control number for each.

## 7. Viewing & printing a form

From the dashboard or Bookings list, tap any row to open its official
**Request of Vehicle** form, then tap **Print / Save as PDF**. The sheet is
sized for long bond (Folio, 8.5×13 in) and prints **two copies per page** with
a cut line between them.

> **Before you print:** in the print window, turn **off** "Headers and footers"
> so the date and web address don't appear on the paper.

## 8. Your profile & password

Choose **Profile** to change your **display name**, **profile photo**, and
**password**. You can also change your **email** — you'll verify the new address
before it takes effect.

## 10. For administrators

Administrators see extra menu items: **Approvals**, **Vehicles**,
**Signatories**, and **Activity History**.

**Approving a request**
1. Open **Approvals** to see all pending requests.
2. Assign a **vehicle**.
3. Choose **Approve** or **Reject**.

**Editing, cancelling, deleting** — on any booking row, administrators can
**Edit** details, **Cancel**, or **Delete**. Admin-only.

**Control numbers** — each booking gets one automatically, like
`2026-07-0229A`: year, month, running number, and a letter that cycles A→Z→A
with each new booking. When entering an old paper record, an administrator can
type the control number by hand instead — including on the printed form.

**Past bookings** — administrators may pick past dates to record trips that
already happened. Regular users can only book today or later.

**Office records**
- **Vehicles** — add, rename, or retire vehicles.
- **Signatories** — set the names, positions, and signature images printed on the form.
- **Activity History** — a log of who created, approved, rejected, or cancelled each booking, and when.

## 11. Common questions

**Can two trips share the same vehicle and time?** The form won't let you pick a
time that's already booked, to avoid mistakes. If a genuine shared trip is
needed, an administrator can arrange it.

**Why can't I choose a vehicle when requesting?** By design — the administrator
assigns the vehicle at approval, based on what's available.

**I can see other people's bookings. Is that right?** Yes — everyone can view
all bookings so the office schedule is transparent. Only administrators can
change them.

**What time zone?** Manila time (Asia/Manila).
